The Story According to Todd

I met Carrie in the break room of Dartmouth Hitchcock when I was introduced to her. She was going to train me on the Robot the following week, and she just lit up and said that we were going to have fun. That was the first time I saw her smile.

I will admit that during the initial courtship I wasn't the most observant of people. When she came back to where we were working and asked if I wanted to play tic-tac-toe, we played a game and then I told her about how if played correctly, it wasn't possible to win that game. What I didn't realize was she was trying to win me.

Further clueless Todd moments happened before we finally had our first date at The Fort in Lebanon, NH. It was after work and I had the Alamo omelette. I remember because I didn't finish half of it because we were talking for hours. We were the only ones there at after midnight other than the server who chimed in "Enjoy it while it lasts." We are enjoying ourselves to this day.
